Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ChoctoTip, a Discord tip bot that enables users to send and receive cryptocurrency tips within Discord servers. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with the bot.

By using ChoctoTip, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

ChoctoTip may collect and store the following types of information to operate effectively:

Discord Information

  • User ID, Username, and Discriminator — used to identify users within Discord.
  • Server (Guild) ID and Channel ID — used for bot commands, logging, and server-specific configurations.
  • Message Content — limited to messages directly interacting with the bot (e.g., command inputs).

Wallet and Transaction Data

  • Wallet Addresses — generated or linked to enable cryptocurrency tipping.
  • Transaction Details — amounts, sender/receiver IDs, and timestamps are stored for logging and verification.

ChoctoTip does not access or store passwords, private keys, or any sensitive data outside what is required to complete transactions.

3. How We Use Your Information

ChoctoTip uses collected data for the following purposes:

  • To process and record cryptocurrency tips.
  • To display balances and transaction histories.
  • To ensure proper functioning of bot commands.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized transactions.
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the bot.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data such as user IDs, wallet addresses, and balances are stored securely in a database.
  • We use encryption and access controls to help protect stored information.
  • While we take steps to safeguard your information, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

ChoctoTip does not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Information may be disclosed only in the following circumstances:

  • Compliance with Law: If required by law or valid legal process.
  • Service Providers: To third-party services (e.g., blockchain APIs) necessary for processing cryptocurrency transactions.
  • Protection of Rights: To prevent fraud or abuse of the bot's services.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide ChoctoTip's functionality. If you remove the bot from your server or request data deletion, related records may be deleted within a reasonable time frame.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent (by discontinuing use of the bot).

To request deletion of your data, contact the ChoctoTip support team through official channels.

8. Third-Party Services

ChoctoTip may integrate with external cryptocurrency services or APIs. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their respective privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of ChoctoTip after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • Discord: via the official ChoctoTip support server https://discord.gg/jmSvm6jn
